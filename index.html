01 · About

Analyst. Scientist. Problem solver.

I'm a performance analyst and sport scientist currently completing an MSc in Applied Sport Performance Analysis at Leeds Beckett University, building on a First Class BSc in Sport and Exercise Science.

My work sits at the intersection of tactical analysis, data science and applied sport science — from coding live ATP tennis matches for broadcast delivery, to building player development frameworks for academy footballers, to conducting applied research on concussion exposure in rugby.

My MSc thesis focuses on offensive and defensive set-piece strategies in the Premier League 2025/26, using Catapult Focus, Wyscout and R to analyse 38 matches across all 20 EPL clubs.
